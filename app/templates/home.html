About Prime Hub Business Services

Prime Hub Business Services is committed to delivering seamless, end-to-end support for businesses across Saudi Arabia. With a focus on precision, compliance, and efficiency, we ensure every stage of your business journey is handled with care.

From company formation and government documentation to Muroor services, TGA approvals, visa processing, payroll, and tax compliance—we serve as your trusted partner for all operational and regulatory needs.

With years of experience and a dedicated team of experts, we simplify complex procedures, eliminate delays, and help your business grow with confidence.

Our Signature Approach

1. Complete Muroor & TGA Services

Expert handling of Muroor services, TGA approvals, vehicle registration, license renewals, transfers, and all vehicle procedures—fast, accurate, and compliant.

2. Government & Legal Services

Specialized in Visa Processing, Commercial Services, Baladiya approvals, and all governmental documentation with precision and reliability.

3. Efficient Business Accounting

Zakat, VAT, billing software, and financial management solutions tailored to meet modern business needs.

4. Complete Business Support

Full-spectrum support including company formation, licensing, visas, and tax compliance for seamless business operations in Saudi Arabia.
